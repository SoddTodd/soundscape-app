Privacy Policy

LIDU Focus System (Public Beta)

Last updated: 2026-04-19

Summary

We keep this app lightweight. Core audio functionality works without analytics consent. If you allow analytics, we use PostHog to understand feature usage and improve the beta.

What We Store Locally

  • Saved presets you create in the app.
  • Your cookie consent preference.
  • Temporary app state used for playback and controls.

Analytics (Optional)

If you accept analytics cookies, PostHog may process usage events such as selected modes, texture choices, timer starts, and feedback-form opens.

  • Purpose: product improvement, UX fixes, and beta prioritization.
  • Legal basis (EU): consent.
  • You can decline analytics and still use the app.

Cookies and Similar Storage

Essential local storage is used for app functionality. Analytics storage is only enabled after consent and can be disabled by declining analytics in the cookie prompt.

Third-Party Services

  • PostHog (analytics, if consent is granted).
  • Tally (feedback form link).
  • Google Fonts (font delivery).

Your Rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you may have rights to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@lidu-system.de
