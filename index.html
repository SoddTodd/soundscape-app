Mood Settings

Stress Relief: Softer, deeper layers to ease tension and calm your mind.

Sleepy Reset: Warmer, gentler textures for low energy moments.

Focus Boost: Cleaner tone with less noise to support concentration.

Calm Balance: Balanced default profile for steady work.

Ambient Textures

No Texture: Pure mode without ambient layers.

Rain Texture: Natural rain sounds for focused work.

Ocean Texture: Gentle waves for calm environments.

Wind Texture: Soft breeze for meditation and relaxation.

Cafe Ambience: Background chatter for creative flow.

Focus Modes

Deep Focus: Minimal movement, narrow frequencies for intense concentration.

Flow State: Broader, airy tones with gentle evolution for creative work.

Energy Boost: Brighter, more active profile when your brain needs a lift.

Binaural: Two close frequencies split left/right for a beat-perception effect.

Binaural Focus: Faster beat for concentration and alertness.

Binaural Relax: Slower beat for calming and unwind sessions.

Controls & Slider

Start Timer: Begin a 25-minute Pomodoro focus cycle with auto-transitions.

Stop: Fade out all active sounds immediately.

Reset: Return timer to default state (25 min focus, Cycle 1).

Skip phase: Jump directly to the next focus/break block when plans change.

Phase Chime: Play a soft cue at every phase transition.

Pause audio on break: Silence the soundscape during breaks, then resume work mode automatically.

Intensity Slider: Adjust volume and movement of the soundscape in real-time.

Solfeggio Overlay

How it Works: Solfeggio plays as an overlay layer, so it can run together with any focus mode.

Blend: Use Blend to control how prominent the Solfeggio layer is in the overall mix.
